Alice

Flowing Blood

Alice casts a blood stream in the designated direction, dealing 400 / 440 / 480 / 520 / 560 / 600 (+120% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies. Use the skill again to immediately teleport to the stream's location. The cooldown for this skill starts after the stream disappears or is used again.


Blood Awe

Alice deals 270 / 295 / 320 / 345 / 370 / 395 (+90% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to surrounding enemies and immobilizing them for 1.2 seconds. Enemies will be slowed by 70% after the immobilization, lasting for 0.8 seconds.




Blood Ode

Alice enters the blood-draining state, continuously consuming 50 / 70 / 90 Mana and dealing 120 / 140 / 160 (+50% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to surrounding enemies every 0.5 seconds and restoring 60 / 70 / 80 (+20% Total Magic Power) HP when hitting heroes - reduced to 50% on non-hero units or 35 / 37.5 / 40 (+10% Total Magic Power) HP.
